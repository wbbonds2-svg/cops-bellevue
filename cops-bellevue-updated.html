Lunch & a Little Learning

With Amy Freeman, Washington State Ombudsman Attorney, on Resident's Rights and the legislative landscape shaping senior housing.

When
Wednesday, May 20th
11:30 AM
Where
Tutta Bella Neapolitan Pizzeria
15600 NE 8th St Suite J1, Bellevue, WA 98008
Speaker
Amy Freeman
Washington State Ombudsman Attorney
Register / RSVP on Eventbrite Email Registration

This Month's Topics

  • Resident's Rights
  • Housing protections in Washington State
  • Discharge and eviction requirements
  • Common compliance risks and how to prevent them
  • Key issues currently impacting seniors

This is essential education for anyone working in senior care. Given current legislative discussions, Amy will briefly address HB 2114 and rent cap considerations — specifically how this may impact independent living residents within mixed assisted living and senior housing communities under the Residential Landlord-Tenant Act.

Est. 1973 · Bellevue, WA

A network built on trust,
education, and care for seniors.

COPS is a volunteer organization founded in 1973 by a group of hospital-based professionals. COPS is now attended by many diverse disciplines — from vendors, case managers, and agencies, to care providers and housing companies.

Our purpose for meeting is to build relationships, share knowledge of our services to support and facilitate patient advocacy, and provide educational presentations for professional development.

COPS meets every other month on the 3rd Wednesday. Anyone is welcome to attend, so feel free to share this invitation with others. We also welcome your suggestions for future guest speakers and any additions to the mailing list.
